## ■ Section 2: Translation Quality Evaluation

### Section Description

Please rate the quality of the English translations shown in the ordering system.
For each item, you will see the original Japanese and its English translation.

**Rating Scale:**

- 5: Perfect translation — terminology is accurate and the expression is natural
- 4: Nearly accurate — minor nuance differences, but meaning is correct
- 3: Understandable — somewhat unnatural, but comprehensible
- 2: Contains errors — some terms are mistranslated, meaning is unclear
- 1: Completely wrong — terms are mistranslated, meaning cannot be understood
